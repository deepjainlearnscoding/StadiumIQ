Emergency Routing

Prepared for Every Scenario

Different emergencies require different responses — CrowdPulse handles them all.

🔥

Fire Emergency

Thermal sensors and CCTV detect smoke. All fans in affected zones re-routed away from fire sources to wind-safe exits.

🌊

Crowd Stampede

Pressure sensors detect surge patterns. Barriers activated, gates redirected, and fans warned before surge propagates.

🏥

Medical Emergency

Fan in distress activates SOS. A clear corridor is automatically maintained around them for the incoming medic team.

⛈️

Weather Event

Lightning strike or severe weather triggers orderly dispersal to covered shelters and internal concourses.
