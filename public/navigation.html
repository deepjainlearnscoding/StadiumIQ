How Navigation Works

CrowdPulse uses a fusion of BLE beacon triangulation and WiFi positioning to pinpoint your location with sub-2m accuracy — even deep inside a concrete stadium where GPS fails.

Technology Stack

Powered by Cutting-Edge Tech

A multi-layer positioning system that works even without internet connectivity.

🔵

BLE Beacons

Bluetooth Low Energy beacons placed every 10m provide precise X/Y positioning throughout all stadium levels.

📡

WiFi Triangulation

Existing stadium WiFi access points are leveraged as a secondary positioning layer for redundancy.

🗺️

Mapbox Indoor

Detailed floor-by-floor indoor map with every corridor, gate, stall, and amenity clearly marked.

🧠

A* Pathfinding

Dynamic shortest-path algorithm that re-routes in real time based on live crowd density from IoT sensors.
